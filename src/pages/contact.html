Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to implement your agricultural technology solutions?

+

Our solutions are customized based on farm size, specific needs, and the technologies implemented. We offer flexible pricing options including outright purchase, leasing, and subscription models. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Do I need internet connectivity on my farm to use your solutions?

+

While internet connectivity enhances the real-time capabilities of our systems, many of our solutions can work with limited connectivity. We offer cellular-based options and local data storage with periodic syncing for farms with connectivity challenges.

How long does installation and setup typically take?

+

Installation timeframes vary based on the complexity of the system and farm size. Basic setups can be completed in 1-2 days, while comprehensive solutions might take 1-2 weeks. We work around your schedule to minimize disruption to farm operations.

Do you provide training for my team?

+

Yes, comprehensive training is included with all our implementations. We provide both on-site and online training options, as well as detailed documentation and ongoing support to ensure your team can effectively use our technology.
